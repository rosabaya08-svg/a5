# Commerce Legal Notice Plan

## Seller Disclosure
Required before product approval:
- Company name, representative name, business registration number.
- Mail-order sales registration number.
- Business address, phone, email, CS contact.
- Return address.

## Product Notice
Required before product approval:
- Product name, model name, manufacturer/importer, country of manufacture.
- Manufacture date, expiration date, or usage period.
- Quality warranty standard.
- A/S owner and contact.

## Shipping, Exchange, Refund, A/S
Required before product approval:
- Shipping fee, remote area fee, dispatch schedule.
- Exchange/return period and restriction reasons.
- Damage/misdelivery handling.
- Refund handling standard.

## Certification/KC
Required when applicable:
- KC target 여부.
- KC certification number.
- Safety certification / safety confirmation / supplier conformity type.
- Children product, electric/living product, food/health/beauty/medical classification.
- Evidence upload mock path.

## Prohibited/Restricted Products
Approval request must be blocked for:
- Guns, swords, explosives, sprays, shockers, crossbows.
- Narcotics, psychotropic drugs, illegal medicine.
- Uncertified electric products and uncertified children products.
- Counterfeit goods, illegal imports, false certification claims.
- Privacy-infringing products and gambling-related products.
- Any legally restricted product.

## Human Review
The app can block obvious red flags, but final legal judgment requires expert review before production operation.
